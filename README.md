Objective : To Transform 2 Million + raw retail transactions into actionable business intelligence using SQL,  
                       PYTHON and DATA VISUALIZATION (POWER BI).
Business Problem : A Major retailer needed to understand the effectiveness of their multi-channel Marketing campaigns and identify high-value customer segments. The goal was to 
Move beyond What was sold and determine why customers buy and how discounts  
Impact the bottom line . 
Key Business Insights:
1)	Top selling component is Misc Items with $93.5k followed by Soft-Drinks with $61.5k.

2)	Age Group of 45-54 involve more in purchasing Misc items and Soft Drinks.

3)	The Grocery Department is more far ahead in generating revenue.

4)	Married People with salaries 50-74k and 35-49k do purchase in bulk.

5)	The Average campaign duration is 46 days

6)	Campaign Lasted 160 Days.

7)	There have been 19 type B campaigns, whose average length was 38 days. In comparison, there have been 6 type C campaigns of 75 days on average.

8)	There are 1584 households participating in the campaign, leaving 916 households who never participated in any campaign.

9)	In Campaigning 18 the maximum number of households participated.

10)	 Most prominent products among coupons created are bathroom products such as hair care and makeup.

11)	 Only 556 coupons have been used.

12)	 The Average Basket Value is $26.93.

13)	 The Most prominents products for which coupons were created are haircare and makeup products,coupons mostly used in food items.

14)	 Sales value increases as Retail discount and coupon discount increases.
